A Demon is one of the worst ghosts to encounter. Being highly aggressive, it will attack more often than any other Ghost, and more violently as well. Treat with extreme caution and attempt to avoid close contact at all costs.

A Jinn is a territorial ghost that will attack when threatened. It has also been known to be able to travel at high speeds through well lit areas, and will do so while hunting distant paranormal investigators. However, cutting off power or turning off lights will limit its speed.

A Spirit is the most common Ghost you will come across, but it is still very powerful and dangerous. It is usually discovered at one of its hunting grounds after an unexplained death. It has no special strengths.

A Wraith is one of the most dangerous ghosts you will find. It is the only known Ghost that has the ability of flight, and has been known to move through doors and walls as well. It's one weakness, however, is that Salt is toxic to a Wraith.

The source of all nightmares, a Mare is a ghost that grows more aggressive in the dark. Keeping the lights on and remaining out of dark areas will limit its aggression. Beware, as it will also attempt to turn the lights off itself.
